About Us

We didn’t plan on starting a cattery. We started as two people who fell completely in love with Maine Coons, and that love kept growing. The line ‘A goal without a plan is just a dream’ stuck with us, and it pushed us to turn our dream into something real. Now we spend our days raising cats that bring us the same warmth, joy, and sense of purpose that brought us here in the first place.
